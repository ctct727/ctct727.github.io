Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

This television series begins after the battle in New York from the first Avengers movie. As the world is changing, there is more and more awareness of superheroes and villains in the real world. To counter, Agent Phil Coulson assembles an elite covert operations team under the S.H.I.E.L.D organization.

Daredevil

In a horrific accident as a child, Matthew Murdock (a.k.a. Daredevil) loses his vision after getting toxic chemicals in them. However, all of his other senses are amplified to the extreme. With these newfound abilities, Daredevil fights to protect his home, Hell's Kitchen, from any and all evil.

Jessica Jones

Jessica Jones, a private investigator with super strength, is on the run from a man known as Killgrave, who has the power to control others. After years of hiding, Killgrave finds Jessica Jones in her neighborhood of Manhattan and attempts to control her mind for her unique abilities.

Luke Cage

A man with unbreakable skin and superhuman strength, Luke Cage joins the fight against crime in New York. Having first appeared in Jessica Jones, there is no telling what he will be doing. The series premieres on Netflix on September 30, 2016.

Cloak and Dagger

Steve Rogers, a rejected military soldier transforms into Captain America after taking a dose of a "Super-Soldier serum". But being Captain America comes at a price as he attempts to take down a war monger and a terrorist organization.

Iron Fist
