Television

Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

This television series begins after the battle in New York from the first Avengers movie. As the world is changing, there is more and more awareness of superheroes and villains in the real world. To counter, Agent Phil Coulson assembles an elite covert operations team under the S.H.I.E.L.D organization.

Daredevil

In a horrific accident as a child, Matthew Murdock (a.k.a. Daredevil) loses his vision after getting toxic chemicals in them. However, all of his other senses are amplified to the extreme. With these newfound abilities, Daredevil fights to protect his home, Hell's Kitchen, from any and all evil.

Jessica Jones

Jessica Jones, a private investigator with super strength, is on the run from a man known as Killgrave, who has the power to control others. After years of hiding, Killgrave finds Jessica Jones in her neighborhood of Manhattan and attempts to control her mind for her unique abilities.

Luke Cage

A man with unbreakable skin and superhuman strength, Luke Cage joins the fight against crime in New York. Having first appeared in Jessica Jones, there is no telling what he will be doing. The series premieres on Netflix on September 30, 2016.

Cloak and Dagger

Cloak; a teenager with a chronic stutter runs away from home after he sees his best friend killed before his eyes. Dagger; a teenage girl from a privileged upbringing runs away from home, tired of being neglected by her supermodel mother. The two meet in New York by coincedence and after being forced to test an illegal drug, the two gain powers and declared war on drug crime. The series is currently in development, but set to debut on Netflix in the near future.

Iron Fist

Iron Fist's powers originate from the mystical city of K'un-L'un. He goes along to find the city as a nine year old boy with his father, mother, and father's best friend Harold Meachum. However, Meachum betrays them and kills Iron Fist's father and his mother dies shortly after. Iron Fist eventually finds the city and learns martial arts there in order to avenge his family. Once mastering his fighting skills, he dips his fist in the molten heart of a dragon giving him iron fists and enhances his other physical abilities.

The Punisher

On a normal afternoon day in Central Park, New York, a shootout between gangs erupts. The Punisher is there with his family and he is the only one who survives. The police don't do a thing because they have deep connections with the gang that caused the incident. In a search for justice, the Punisher decides to take it into his own hands. This series will follow the Punisher after the events of Daredevil season 2.

The Defenders

Daredevil, Jessica Jones, Luke Cage, and Iron Fist, team up to defend New York City. So far there have been no details about the series as Luke Cage and Iron Fist have not even debuted yet. It is suspected that the main antagonist will be Kingpin as he continues to corrupt the city they all love and seek to protect.
