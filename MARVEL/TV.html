MARVEL


Phase 1: Avengers Assembled

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a billionaire genius and inventor, realizes the true nature of his destructive weapons after having them used against him in Afghanistan. To stop the abuse of his weapons, he creates a super suit to become Iron Man.

The Incredible Hulk

Bruce Banner, a scientist on the run from the U.S. Government, must find a cure for the monster he emerges whenever he loses his temper. However, Banner then must fight a soldier whom unleashes himself as a threat stronger than he.

Iron Man 2

With the world now aware of his identity as Iron Man, Tony Stark's life has not been nearly the same. While dealing with a deadly personal health problem, Tony must battle with bad guys seeking to copy his technology as well as the government who are concerned that one man has so much power.

Thor

The powerful but arrogant god and prince of Asgard, Thor is banished to live amongst humans in Midgard (Earth). He must find what it truly means to be a king to return home and protect his kingdom from impending evil.

Captain America: The First Avenger

Steve Rogers, a rejected military soldier transforms into Captain America after taking a dose of a "Super-Soldier serum". But being Captain America comes at a price as he attempts to take down a war monger and a terrorist organization.

The Avengers

Led by SHIELD and Nick Fury: Iron Man, The Incredible Hulk, Captain America, Black Widow, Hawkeye, and Thor; Earth's mightiest heroes must come together and learn to fight as a team if they are to stop the mischievous Loki and his alien army from enslaving humanity.
