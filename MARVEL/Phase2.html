MARVEL


Phase 2: Universe Expansion

Iron Man 3

Plagued with worry and insomnia since saving New York from destruction, Tony Stark, now, is more dependent on the suits that give him his Iron Man persona -- so much so that every aspect of his life is affected, including his relationship with Pepper. After a malevolent enemy known as the Mandarin reduces his personal world to rubble, Tony must rely solely on instinct and ingenuity to avenge his losses and protect the people he loves.

Thor: The Dark World

In ancient times, the species known as the Dark Elves used their ultimate weapon - the Aether - in a war with Asgard. After losing the war, the Aether was taken and hidden. The Dark Elves return and hopes to find it and destroy the Nine Realms and only Thor can stop them.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

S.H.I.E.L.D. has now become a bigger and more involved agency after the events in New York from the Avengers. Captain America soon finds out that all is not as it seems and after Nick Fury is ambushed, he seeks to find the truth behind S.H.I.E.L.D. Along with Black Widow and new ally Falcon, secrets are discovered and Captain America must face is toughest foe yet.

Guardians of the Galaxy

Brash space adventurer Peter Quill finds himself the quarry of relentless bounty hunters after he steals an orb coveted by Ronan, a powerful villain. To evade Ronan, Quill is forced into an uneasy truce with four disparate misfits: gun-toting Rocket Raccoon, treelike-humanoid Groot, enigmatic Gamora, and vengeance-driven Drax the Destroyer. But when he discovers the orb's true power and the cosmic threat it poses, Quill must rally his ragtag group to save the universe.

Avengers: Age of Ultron

In an attempt to harness the power of an infinity stone, Tony Stark creates the ultimate artificial intelligence known as Ultron. Unfortunately, Ultron's ideas of saving the world and Tony's are vastly different. Ultron seeks to destroy the human race like a reset button, but the Avengers will not allow that to happen without a fight.

Ant-Man

Scott Lang, one of the best cat burglars out there, gets caught and arrested. Behind his criminal ways Hank Pym sees talent and a good heart and decides to pass on the Ant-Man suit to Lang. With the ability to shrink and grow, Ant-Man must stop Darren Cross (Yellowjacket) from selling the same dangerous technology to the wrong people.
