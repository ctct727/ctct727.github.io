MARVEL


Phase 3: Infinity War

Captain America: Civil War

In light of the recent disasters and destruction caused by the heroes, the world government has decided to take steps to prevent further damage. The Sokovia Accords are written to limit all superheroes but a difference in beliefs between Captain America and Iron Man lead to severe conflict within the team.

Doctor Strange

Stephen Strange, a brilliant but arrogant surgeon, becomes partially paralyzed after a car crash. He loses the ability to use his hands and in search of fulfillment after the accident, he finds the Ancient One, a sorcerer who will teach him the ways of magic to fight evil.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2

Peter Quill (a.k.a. Star Lord), Groot, Rocket Racoon, Drax, and Gamora all return to continue their journey causing trouble and exploring space. The film is likely to feature Nebula as the main villain and will not revolve around the two yet-to-be-revealed infinity stones.

Spiderman: Homecoming

As the movie is a long time away from being released, most speculate Homecoming will be a coming of age story about Peter Parker (Spiderman) as he juggles his high school life with being a superhero. It is also believed that the main villain will be the Vulture.

Thor: Ragnarok

So far nothing has been completely confirmed for the god of thunder's third film. Mark Ruffalo has been added to the cast which likely means Hulk will be sharing the screen with Thor for some portion of the film. Rumors say Cate Blanchett will play Hela, the goddess of death and that the film will be pivotal in leading to the Infiniy War.

Black Panther

He is the prince of the nation of Wkanda, the only place where the rare metal, Vibranium, can be found.The Black Panther is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and wears a suit embedded with vibranium. At the end of Civil War, we see Black Panther taking care of Captain America and Bucky in his home country. It is speculated the Black Panther movie will have Thanos attack Wkanda which will lead to a greater conflict.

Avengers: Infinity War Part 1

Still in the earliest stages of development, there is just about zero information on anything in this movie. The majority of heroes are expected to appear and have an impact in their own way somehow in the film. The directors will be Joe and Anthony Russo who also directed Captain America: Civil War and Captain America: The Winter Soldier.

Ant-Man and the Wasp

With the introduction of Hope Van Dyne as the Wasp at the end of the Ant-Man movie, the two will take the screen together. Nothing at all has been revealed besides the title. It is likely to focus on the two heroes searching for the original wasp in the quantum realm. However, if the title is a reference to the original comics, then it will feature an alien-criminal as the main villain.

Captain Marvel

Originally known as Ms. Marvel, she has many superpowers such as super strength, ability to fly, ability to absorb and project energy, and some precognitive abilities. Her powers are from when she accidentally got caught in an alien explosive device while in the US Air Force. This film will not be an origin story.

Avengers: Infinity War Part 2

In the grand finale and culmination of ten years worth of big-budget films by Marvel, the Avengers all come together to take on their ultimate nemesis Thanos. The film at the moment has casted more than 60 main roles to be in the movie.Every hero still alive is expected to make an appearance as they take on Thanos, played by Josh Brolin, who will most likely have all six infinity stones by this time.
